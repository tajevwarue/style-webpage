ROYALTY

What's better than a bright clear sky?
The response will differ from different people.
But when the sky is asked you'd always hear it say:

A smile from Royalty makes me smile a bright sky.
A tear from Royalty makes me rain.
A word from Royalty makes me sunny and
A frown from her makes me cloudy.
At night she's the stars that decorate me.
And at dawn the sun that rises from the east.

Please Royalty, smile on through the day as your smile brings joy
not only to those around but every one on the earth
as it makes our sky bright and clear.
