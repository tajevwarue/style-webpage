YOU AND I

On the roads ever travelled
On the seas ever sailed
In the air flown by many
You and I always together

In the heat of the day
In the cool of the evening
Underneath the shining moon
You and I always together

In the castle of the great
In the mansion of the lofty
Inside all rooms therein
You and I always together

With words ever spoken
With gestures ever made
With feelings ever felt
You and I always together
Glue to the eyes
Glue to the lips
Every warmth being shared
You and I always together

You and I now
You and I ever
You and I will be
You and I always together
